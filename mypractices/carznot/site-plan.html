Overview

Purpose

This is a restricted website intended specifically for automotive dealers to help them find used vehicles by working with each other.

This is done by having dealers list vehicles on the site that they have an immediate need for, allowing other dealers to respond with inventory they are willing to sell wholesale.

It will also allow automotive dealers to post vehicles on the site that are overage, or off-make, that the dealer is willing to trade to another dealer.

Audience
