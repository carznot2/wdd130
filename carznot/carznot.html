The Kicking Horse!

Near Golden, British Columbia

This is one of the fastest rivers in North America for White Water Refting! It is wild and is fun and guaranteed to make you want for more as you bounce your way along through narrow gorges and wild rapids, people sceaming, yelling out, and holding on! We guarantee the best and we deliver! The fast flowing churning waters of the Kicking Horse have all that you might ever dream of! Come check it out for yourselves! We guarantee that you will not be disappointed!

The Chilquat River!

Near Revelstoke, British Columbia

This is what movies are made of, the wild, non-relenting Chilquat River, the wildlife, the hiking trails and the camping! Every moment spent is a moment you'll enjoy! Seven miles of wild water, raging currents, scenery that you will not forget and will see nowhere else. Our guides as you see here in the photo are like no others! They'll make you laugh and at times they will also make you scream, and at times you will wonder if you might survive. Enjoy the thrills and enjoy the very best there is on the Chilquat!

The Ninkumpuk River!

In Southern British Columbia, near Fernie

This has to be the very best river in the world to take your family! With safety as our priority they will all be safe, but will also enjoy white water rafting on a river with perhaps the most spectacular scenery in the world! Our world class campground has a pool as well for children and for adults and there are hiking trails as well fishing spots where we know you will not be disapointed! Enjoy a spectacular day on the river, then go fishing and enjoy the smell of your fish frying in a pan on an open fire! The Ninkmumpuk has it all. It's a popular place to bring the family so don't forget to book your camping spot in advance!
