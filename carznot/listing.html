$49 Listing Fee!

That's all you pay! - A dealer buying your vehicle pays us nothing!

Instead of sending a vehicle to the auction you can list vehicles here for trade or sale to other dealers.

Vehicles might include over-age inventory, off-make trades that might not fit into your inventory, or specialty commercial vehicles or RVs. The fee charged is per month and allows you to list one vehicle on the site at a time, however if that vehicle is sold you can change that listing to show a different vehicle at no extra charge.

Should a dealer want to show two vehicles on the site, at a time, the fee increases to $89 per month. Remember as well, that valuations can differ from one region to another. The website allows you to take advantage of those differences and you might get a better price from a dealer in another region or across the border, than you would from a customer in your area.
